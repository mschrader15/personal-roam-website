Stoic Clock

  • What is it

    • The idea for the stoic clock arose from a need to be reminded of my own mortality. I wanted to create a timer -- one to remind me that my time was finite, and that would look good doing it. The result was a Nixie Tube clock that uses my projected death age to display a countdown of my days remaining. As of writing this I have 23487 days to go...Let's make the most of them! The clock isn't always so morbid, as it also displays the current time.
      • Users can configure when and how often the death countdown is displayed at https://stoic-clock-website.herokuapp.com/
      • days till death = average age @ death of a person of my demographics + my DOB - today's date
      • I have some extra PCB's laying around, so if you want to talk about me building you one, reach out!

  • The mechanics of it

    • A Raspberry Pi is the brains. Nixie Tubes provide the numbers.
